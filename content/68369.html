"Adverse events” are unfavorable or unusual reactions/effects/illnesses that can occur with the use of some dietary supplements.

Be sure to listen to your body and pay attention to how you feel. Keep a written list of all prescription medications, dietary supplement products, and over-the-counter medications you may be taking.

Tell your healthcare provider about any changes in your health status, particularly if you start to feel symptoms you did not experience prior to taking the supplement(s).
