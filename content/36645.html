Third-Party Certification
Remember:
 
  • Third-party certification organizations provide independent testing to evaluate supplements and their ingredients.
     
  • Third-party testing does not guarantee product effectiveness or safety.
     
  • Certification programs can verify product quality and confirm that the ingredients match the product label.
     
  • Third-party testing is a snapshot in time. Future product batches may not be the same as the batch that was evaluated.
