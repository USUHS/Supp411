OPSS maintains a High-Risk Supplement List as an educational resource for service members. The products on this list ARE NOT BANNED because DoD does not approve or ban dietary supplements. The list is to help you learn to RECOGNIZE products that could contain stimulants, steroids, or other ingredients that could present health and/or sport anti-doping risks.
 
If you are considering using a dietary supplement, you should visit the OPSS High-Risk Supplement List first. If your product is on the list, that doesn’t mean you can’t use it. However, it does mean you should consider whether you are comfortable with the risks that product poses to you. Ultimately, only you can decide what is right for you.
