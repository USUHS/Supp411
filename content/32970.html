Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) Logos
By law, companies are required to manufacture dietary supplement products according to Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP), but many of them do not follow the law.

Just because you see a GMP stamp, that does not mean the product is safe.

You cannot draw conclusions about the safety of the supplement based on the inclusion of a GMP stamp.

FDA does not issue stamps of approval for GMP.
