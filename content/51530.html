Step 3: Look for Product Warnings
Realize  Recognize  Reduce
Has FDA or OPSS issued a warning about the product in question? Visit OPSS and the OPSS High-Risk Supplement List to find out.

The FDA Tainted Weight Loss Products, Tainted Body Building Products, and Tainted Sexual Enhancement Products pages also lists specific dietary supplement product warnings. 

If yes, this may be a risky product. Look for a safer alternative.

If you can't find any specific warnings, move on to the next step.
