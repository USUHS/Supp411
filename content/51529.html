Step 2: Look for Third-Party Certification
Look for third-party certification, but also evaluate the limits of each program. No program is perfect, and certification is not a guarantee that the product is safe or free from prohibited substances.

If your product in question is not certified by a third-party agency, ask yourself:

Is there an equivalent certified product you can use?

If there are no food alternatives and no certified equivalents, then move on to the next step.
