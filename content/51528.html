Consult a healthcare provider and/or dietitian to make sure there is a CLEAR NUTRITIONAL BENEFIT in using a supplement and that there are no food alternatives.

No clear nutritional benefit? Then why are you taking the dietary supplement? No need to go further in this process. This product is not the right supplement for you.
