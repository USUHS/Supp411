Proprietary Blends
Why do companies use proprietary blends? FDA requires supplements to list the amount of each ingredient UNLESS the ingredients are part of a proprietary blend.
 
  • It can be risky to use products with proprietary blends because you don’t know how much of each ingredient you are getting.
     
  • This is especially important for proprietary blends with stimulant or stimulant-like ingredients, such as caffeine, synephrine, or yohimbine.
     
  • The only way to know what is in any particular dietary supplement product is through laboratory testing, which is why it is important to look for third-party certification.
