DoD Banned Substances
The Department of Defense (DoD) does not have a “black list” for dietary supplements, nor does it have any official “approved list.” In general, DoD follows FDA and DEA guidelines with regard to whether a substance is allowed for use as a dietary supplement or dietary supplement ingredient.
