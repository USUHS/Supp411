Summary
The following main points summarize this tutorial.
 
  • The supplement industry is vast, and there is no guarantee that supplement labels are accurate or that the available products are safe.
 
  • FDA has identified four high-risk supplement categories: bodybuilding, weight loss, sexual enhancement, and diabetes. Products from these categories, as well as energy drinks, may put you at a higher health risk. 
  
  • If you decide to use supplements, use the risk-reduction steps to help  avoid consuming illegal or unhealthy products. 
 
1. Seek professional advice.
2. Look for third-party certification.
3. Look for product warnings.
4. Evaluate the label for red flags.
