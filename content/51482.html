It is true that:
The product label is not a reliable indicator of what is in a supplement. Some products labeled as dietary supplements can contain pharmaceutical drugs. The Department of Defense (DoD) does not have a “black list” for dietary supplements, nor does it have any official “approved list.” Please see the OPSS FAQ on HPRC’s website, which is pertinent to all service branches. It includes examples of banned substances and links to more information.
 
You should be aware that bodybuilding supplements can contain steroids or other ingredients that are not listed on the label. Pay attention to information in this tutorial to learn more about the risks involved with bodybuilding supplements.
