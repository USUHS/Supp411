Step 4: Evaluate the Label for Red Flags
Evaluate the product for yourself by looking for red flags such as the ones listed below.
 
  • There are prohibited substances listed on the label.
     
  • The product is in the bodybuilding, weight loss, diabetes, or sexual enhancement category.
     
  • There is a warning that the product may be banned in some sports, or there is a health warning.
     
  • The product advertises to treat or cure a disease (for example, the common cold, diabetes, arthritis).
     
  • The product advertises to be “all natural” but to have the same effects as a prohibited substance (for example, testosterone, steroids, growth hormone, IGF-1, or EPO).
     
  • The product lists a proprietary blend.
     
  • The product has trademarked ingredients, but you can’t find good information
    about the ingredients.
     
  • The product advertises to be approved by FDA.

If you find one or more red flags, it may mean the
product is risky.
