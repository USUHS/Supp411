Does DoD ban supplements?
The Department of Defense (DoD) does not maintain an official list of dietary supplement products that are either "allowed" or "banned."

However, the Food and Drug Administration (FDA), an agency responsible for protecting and promoting public health in the U.S., has found that many dietary supplements contain undeclared drug ingredients.

Some of these drug ingredients can cause serious health problems and can cause you to test positive on a military drug test.
